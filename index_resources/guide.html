Welcome to Norrland! We are the largest Nordic-themed region in NationStates, and we have an amazing community of active players who can help introduce you to the game and explain how our region works.

Our region has a thriving roleplay community with lots of international events that you can get involved in or choose to stay out of, depending on your nation's politics. We have military relations, trade alliances, space missions, and more. To begin, follow the steps below:

  1. Register for roleplay by visiting this page.
  2. Take a look at the Regional Map and get an idea of the territory you would like.
  3. Fill out the Map Application Form with a detailed description of the area you would like. Make sure it isn't already taken.
  4. Use the Military Calculator to organize your military to be used in roleplay.

If you have any other questions, feel free to ask on the RMB or send a telegram to Solborg.
