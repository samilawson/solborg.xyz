# COUP: Third Faction?

Posted by: slendergrad

Nations involved: Slendergrad, Mysterious Nation

In a twist of fate, Evan Lee, who is also a relative of Archer and the late Anthony Lee, took control of parts of the country with the help of a sizable armed force. The relative, who happened to be the Chairman of the National Development Party (which has been ruling the nation since its founding in 1863), has made his ambitions for the presidency loud and clear. However, there are rumours that Evan only fought against the coup because Archer refused to make him Vice President.
